About

I'm a passionate, purpose-driven, Frontend/UX Developer that is happily married to the most wonderful woman, is a coffee enthusiast, loves to solve problems and find solutions, and serves a gracious, forgiving, and loving Jesus.

I'd like to break down "purpose-driven". I’m passionate about developing an online presence for individuals and companies to give them exactly what they need in the crazy digital world we live in. More so than just building a website or developing a feature, to simply be able to serve someone, an entire company, and even more so, a company's target audience, and allow them to live a better and more efficient life from what I’ve developed...that's what my self-purpose drives me to accomplish.

I am proficient in Frontend technologies such as HTML, Bootstrap, and CSS, have solid fundemental knowledge of vanilla JavaScript and jQuery, and a basic, beginners, understanding of PHP. Being a professional learner and being apart of an industry where programming technologies are forever evolving, I am constantly perfecting my craft as a Developer.
